About DroneClear

DroneClear Configurator is an enterprise-grade internal tool for managing FPV drone components, validating build compatibility, creating step-by-step assembly guides, and maintaining complete audit trails. The platform supports over 3,000 real components across 12 categories, with a real-time compatibility engine that checks mounting patterns, voltage ranges, connector types, and more. Every build session is tracked with serial numbers, timestamped events, and SHA-256 verified photo evidence for airworthiness documentation.
